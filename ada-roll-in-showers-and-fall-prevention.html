Falls are the #1 cause of injury for older adults over age 65. Bathrooms are one of the most hazardous spaces because of slippery surfaces, limited mobility, and hard-to-navigate layouts. (CDC)

Why ADA roll in showers matter

  • Zero-threshold entry eliminates tripping hazard
  • Seating options make bathing doable with low energy
  • Grab bars add stability
  • Better for wheelchairs & walkers

Staying independent

Being able to shower safely often means being able to stay at home longer. Roll in showers support aging-in-place goals and help seniors avoid hospitalizations caused by fall injuries.
